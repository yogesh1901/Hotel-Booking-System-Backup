Dashboard

Understanding Your Dashboard

Key Metrics

  • Total Bookings: Shows current active reservations
  • Revenue: Displays total earnings (last month comparison)
  • Occupancy Rate: Percentage of rooms currently occupied
  • Pending Feedback: Number of unprocessed guest reviews

Charts

  • Monthly Revenue: 6-month revenue trend visualization
  • Occupancy Rate: Graphical representation of room status

Tip: Click on any chart segment to view detailed data for that period.
